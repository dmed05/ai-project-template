# Privacy Policy

This repository is intended for public portfolio work.

## Never Commit

- real customer, employee, vendor, account, or site information;
- live schedules, routes, access instructions, contracts, or invoices;
- production exports, source emails, screenshots, photos, PDFs, or recordings;
- addresses, phone numbers, private email addresses, or contact notes;
- API keys, passwords, tokens, certificates, or environment files;
- private local paths, internal URLs, or copied operational text; or
- unsupported business-impact claims.

## Safe Portfolio Content

- generic process and architecture descriptions;
- synthetic fixtures that cannot be mapped to real operations;
- tested reusable code;
- human-review and safety boundaries;
- supported qualitative benefits; and
- measured results only when the measurement can be explained publicly.

When uncertain, exclude the information and describe the process at a higher
level.
